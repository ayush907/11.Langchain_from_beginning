Purani Haweli:
Ek sheher ke bahar ek purani haweli thi, jiska naam Kali Bhoot Bangla tha.
Log us haweli se door rehte the, kyunki wahan anjaani awaazen sunayi deti thi, jaise koi chal raha ho par nazar na aaye.

Arjun ka Dare:
Arjun, ek college student, apne doston ke saath haweli ke baare mein sun raha tha.
Doston ne dare ke bola, "Tu jaake wahan raat guzaar ke dekh."
Arjun ne unki baat maani aur raat ko haweli ki taraf chal diya.

Haweli mein Entry:
Arjun jab haweli ke andar gaya, toh thandi hawa chal rahi thi.
Pura mahal suna tha. Wo apna flashlight jala ke andar ghusa, lekin ek ajeeb si bechaini thi.

Sheeshe Ke Kamre Mein:
Arjun ek purana sheeshe wala kamra dekhta hai.
Wahan usne apne chehre ko dekha, lekin woh uska chehra nahi tha, koi aur tha.

Bhoot Ki Awas:
Arjun ne apne peeche dekha aur dekha ek purani aurat khadi thi, baal khule, safed kapde pehene wali.
Aurat ne apni aankhon se Arjun ko dekha aur apni ungli se usse chup karne ka ishara kiya.

Bhaag Kar Escape:
Arjun ka dil tez dhadakne laga, aur wo bhaag gaya.
Jab wo wahan se nikla, sab darwaze band ho gaye the. Koi madad nahi aayi.

Final Encounter:
Phir wohi aurat khaamoshi mein uske samne khadi ho gayi thi.
Aur wo ek saaya ban gayi, jo Arjun ke aage chal raha tha.
Arjun ki aankhein band hui aur wo haweli ka hissa ban gaya... ek aurat ke rooh ke saath.
