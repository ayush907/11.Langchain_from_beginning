Rait ki zameen pe fareeb ke yeh saare pal,
Inmein rehne waale tum ho... ya kal?
Safed dikhta patthar, par bhar gaya hai gham se,
Zimma hai kudrat ka, ya phir hum se?

Saja hamaari hai, khata chahe kiski bhi ho,
Raahon mein andhera ho, ya roshni bhi ho.
Jisko jo bhi milta hai, besabab nahi milta,
Mujhse bole dil mera — sabko sab nahi milta.

Kagazon ke sheher mein sapne jalte hain,
Jhooth ke taj mein sach bhi palte hain.
Jo bola maine, wo suna kisne tha?
Khud se ladta raha, wajah poochta raha.

Zakhm mile lekin chehra muskaan mein tha,
Dil rota tha par duniya to armaan mein tha.
Chhoti si khushi thi, lekin usmein dard bhi tha,
Khaali tha sab kuch, phir bhi kuch tha...

Mitti ke insaan, mitti mein mil jaayenge,
Sawaalon ke jungle mein khud ko bhool jaayenge.
Kya yehi kismet hai, ya likha gaya jhoot tha?
Jo likha tha maine, wo tera bhi toh roop tha.

Saja hamaari hai, kyun? Ye sawaal rehta hai,
Har raat ka khwab, adhoora sa kehta hai.
Jisko jo bhi milta hai, besabab nahi milta,
Mujhse bole dil mera — sabko sab nahi milta.

Aaj bhi kahin vo awaaz ghoomti hai,
Jo sach tha kabhi, ab dhoondhti hai.
Pattharon ke sheher mein koi ro raha tha,
Aur khud ko khud se chhupa raha tha.

Saja hamaari hai… yeh likh lo kahani mein,
Kuch to tha hummein… jo reh gaya kahin paani mein.
